Cookie Policy

This cookie policy explains how DM Design & Build LTD uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. It should be read alongside our privacy policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites work properly, remember certain preferences, improve performance and provide information about how visitors use the site.

How we may use cookies

We may use cookies or similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • To help the website function correctly
  • To improve security and site performance
  • To remember basic preferences where relevant
  • To understand how visitors use the website
  • To support contact forms or other website features provided through third-party services

Types of cookies we may use

Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to operate properly. They may support core site functions such as page navigation, security, form handling or access to basic website features.

Performance and analytics cookies

These cookies may help us understand how visitors use the website, such as which pages are visited most often and whether visitors experience errors. This information helps improve the website and user experience.

Functional cookies

These cookies may allow the website to remember choices you make, such as certain preferences, in order to provide a more tailored browsing experience.

Third-party cookies

Some features on our website may rely on third-party providers, such as website hosting, embedded content, or enquiry form services. These providers may place cookies or use similar technologies where necessary for their service to function.

Cookie consent

Where required, we will ask for your consent before using non-essential cookies. Strictly necessary cookies do not usually require consent because they are needed for the website to function properly.

If a cookie banner or consent tool is active on the website, you can use it to manage your cookie preferences.

How to control cookies

You can usually control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored on your device
  • Block some or all cookies
  • Delete cookies already stored
  • Set preferences for certain websites

Please note that blocking some cookies may affect how the website works and could limit certain features or functionality.

Changes to this cookie policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes to our website, the cookies we use, legal requirements or the services we rely on. Please check this page occasionally for the latest version.
