Our lawful bases for processing

Under UK data protection law, we rely on one or more lawful bases depending on why we are using your information.

  • Contract: where we need your information to provide a quote, arrange work, or deliver agreed services.
  • Legitimate interests: where it is reasonable for us to use your information to run and improve our business, respond to enquiries and keep records.
  • Legal obligation: where we must keep certain records for tax, accounting or legal reasons.
  • Consent: where consent is needed for certain non-essential cookies or marketing activity, if used.

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only where necessary with trusted service providers that help us run our business and website.

  • Website hosting and technical service providers

How long we keep your information

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose it was collected, including to respond to enquiries, manage projects, maintain records, and comply with legal, tax or insurance requirements.

If you contact us for a quote but do not go ahead, we may keep your enquiry details for a reasonable period for administration, follow-up and record-keeping. If you become a customer, we may retain relevant records for longer where required for accounting, contractual or legal reasons.
